Carlos Agredano
b. 1998 Los Angeles, CA
Carlos Agredano utilizes readymade and process-based sculptures to materialize issues of race and inequity, particularly within the context of American urban planning systems. Employing toxic, ephemeral, and site-specific material, Agredano’s work exposes how the design and organization of city space perpetuate disparity across Los Angeles.
